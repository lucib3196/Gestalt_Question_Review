$$ \sigma_{max} = \frac{M}{S} \\ $$

  • \( S = \frac{b \cdot h^{2}}{6} \) = section modulus of the beam

And the section modulus \( S \) for a rectangular beam is given by: $$ S = \frac{b \cdot h^{2}}{6} $$

$$ \sigma_{max} = \frac{M}{S} = \frac{[[params.intermediate.M]]}{\frac{[[params.width]] \cdot [[params.height]]^{2}}{6}} \\ = \frac{6 \cdot [[params.intermediate.M]]}{[[params.width]] \cdot [[params.height]]^{2}} \\ = \frac{6 \cdot \frac{[[params.load]] \cdot [[params.span]]}{4}}{[[params.width]] \cdot [[params.height]]^{2}} \\ = \frac{6 \cdot [[params.load]] \cdot [[params.span]]}{4 \cdot [[params.width]] \cdot [[params.height]]^{2}} \\ $$
